WHAT HAPPENS NEXT?

Once you join, you’ll receive a confirmation and welcome email, and your school’s color on the leaderboard will change, showing that your account is activated and you're ready to begin Level 1 - Activate.

After that, you’re all set to log and:login and:

  • Choose your team name and select a fun team avatar
  • Upload your progress to move through the Challenge levels
  • Earn bonus points to help your school win exciting awards

BONUS:Every school that joins the Challenge will get a FREE Teacher Guide and Pupil Challenge Workbook, filled with fact sheets and step-by-step worksheets to guide you through the Plastic Shoonya journey!

Aarna & Milo will also be hosting webinars and workshops to make sure all schools feel fully supported on their mission.

For more details, check out our guide on How to Go Plastic Shoonya School!
